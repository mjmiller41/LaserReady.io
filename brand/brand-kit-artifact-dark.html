06 · Voice & tone

Unchanged. Tone doesn’t have a theme.

Maker-to-maker, name the mechanism, never imply a clean cut. The dark surface only changes how the words are lit, not what they say.

✓ We say

  • “Open paths — the #1 cause of parts that won’t release.”
  • “Two spans are under 0.8 mm — they may burn through 3 mm ply.”
  • “‘The file is good’ and ‘the cut is perfect’ are different promises. We make the first one.”

✕ We never say

  • “AI-powered laser optimization that guarantees perfect cuts every time.”
  • “Upload your design and let our magic do the rest ✨”
  • Vague reassurance with no mechanism, no number, no named check.

07 · A first-class brand element

The honest box: a light stamp on dark

In dark mode the heavy ink border inverts to a heavy ink-200 border on the surface — still stamped and deliberate, never a muted footnote. Copy is verbatim.

The honest part

What we won’t pretend.

We can’t see your laser. Power, speed, focus, and the board you loaded are yours to dial in. We guarantee the file is structurally sound — closed, clean, correctly scaled and layered. That’s the promise we can keep.
